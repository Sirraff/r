Job Interview Workshop Guide for Undergrad & Newgrads

Welcome to my comprehensive Interview Workshop guide! I created this workshop to support fellow Computer Science majors who are navigating the challenges of securing undergraduate internships and entry-level jobs in the tech industry.

I’ve applied to over 300 job positions, faced countless rejections, and learned from my mistakes through trial and error. This workshop is my way of helping you avoid those pitfalls and fast-track your journey to landing your dream role.

What’s Included:

  • A collection of carefully curated resources, including links to top platforms like NeetCode and LeetCode.
  • Recorded workshop videos covering essential techniques, from tackling coding problems to presenting yourself confidently in HR interviews.
  • A structured study plan for progressing through topics step-by-step, starting with basics and moving toward advanced problems.
  • My personal insights and strategies to stay motivated, avoid burnout, and make consistent progress.

Why This Workshop?

This guide combines everything I’ve learned from my own experience into a practical, actionable format that can help you:

  • Master algorithms and data structures efficiently without feeling overwhelmed.
  • Learn how to apply the STAR method to present your experiences effectively in behavioral interviews.
  • Avoid common mistakes, from poorly formatted resumes to bad time management during interview prep.

Whether you’re preparing for your first technical interview or trying to land a challenging internship, this workshop will provide you with the tools and strategies to stand out in a competitive job market.
